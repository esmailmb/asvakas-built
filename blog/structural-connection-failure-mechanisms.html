Structural Connection Failure Mechanisms

Understanding failure mechanisms is critical to preventing structural issues.

Common Failures

  • Shear failure
  • Tension rupture
  • Anchor pull-out
  • Block shear
  • Connection slip

Causes

  • Undersized connections
  • Improper installation
  • Design errors
  • Material defects

Engineering Insight

Failure often initiates at the weakest link in the load path.
