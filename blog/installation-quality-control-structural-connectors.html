Installation and Quality Control of Structural Connectors

Proper installation is essential to achieving design performance.

Key Factors

  • Correct fasteners
  • Proper alignment
  • Adequate spacing
  • Compliance with specifications

Quality Control

Inspection should verify:

  • Fastener type and quantity
  • Installation method
  • Alignment and positioning

Risks of Improper Installation

  • Reduced capacity
  • Premature failure
  • Safety risks

Asvakas Role

We provide construction-phase support and inspection services to ensure proper installation and performance.
