Structural Retrofit and Rehabilitation Engineering

Structural retrofit involves modifying existing structures to improve strength, stiffness, or load-carrying capacity.

Drivers for Retrofit

  • Code upgrades
  • Change in occupancy
  • Structural deterioration
  • Seismic deficiencies

Engineering Challenges

  • Existing material properties
  • Unknown conditions
  • Load redistribution
  • Compatibility with existing systems

Retrofit Strategies

  • Adding reinforcement
  • Strengthening connections
  • Installing new lateral systems
  • Enhancing anchorage

Performance Objectives

  • Life safety
  • Damage control
  • Immediate occupancy (in some cases)

Asvakas Role

Asvakas Engineering develops retrofit strategies that balance safety, cost, and constructability.
