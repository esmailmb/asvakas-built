Seismic Design of Structural Connections

Seismic forces impose dynamic and cyclic loading conditions that require special design considerations.

Characteristics of Seismic Loading

  • Reversed cyclic loading
  • High deformation demands
  • Energy dissipation requirements

Design Philosophy

  • Ductility
  • Redundancy
  • Controlled failure mechanisms

Connection Requirements

  • Allow deformation without brittle failure
  • Maintain load path continuity
  • Resist cyclic degradation

Asvakas Role

We design connections that meet seismic performance objectives and code requirements.
