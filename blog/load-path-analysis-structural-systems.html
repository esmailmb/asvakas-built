Load Path Analysis in Structural Systems

Load path analysis ensures that forces are transmitted through a continuous and predictable route from point of application to foundation.

Concept

  • Load collection
  • Load transfer through members
  • Transfer through connections
  • Delivery to foundation

Discontinuities

Interruptions in load paths can cause:

  • Force concentration
  • Local overstress
  • Structural failure

Engineering Approach

  • Gravity load paths
  • Lateral load paths
  • Redundancy in systems

Asvakas Role

We analyze and verify load paths to ensure structural integrity and performance under all load conditions.
