Overview

System-level work is where approvals, detailing, and real site conditions come together.

Ontario projects often become difficult at the interfaces: balcony rehabilitation tied to exterior wall work, temporary works tied to active construction sequencing, heritage fabric tied to modern interventions, or field repairs that must satisfy both code and constructability.

Use this page when the project needs targeted engineering around an assembly, interface, existing-condition constraint, or specialty construction requirement rather than a broad general scope alone.

Why It Matters

Better systems coordination reduces risk before it turns into field delay or repair scope.

Targeted systems consulting helps define assumptions early, align engineering with approvals, and reduce avoidable issues tied to sequencing, moisture, durability, access, and existing-condition uncertainty.

Permit alignment
