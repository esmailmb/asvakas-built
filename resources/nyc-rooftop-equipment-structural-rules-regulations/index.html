NYC Rooftop Equipment Structural Rules and Regulations

A high-level guide to rooftop support framing, dunnage, code triggers, energy-code context, zoning, and inspection-sensitive details in NYC.

Public starting points

The most useful public anchors are the 2022 Construction Codes, the 2025 Energy Conservation Code, DOB NOW: Build, the DOB NOW Public Portal, special inspections guidance, and the NYC Zoning Resolution where screening or rooftop bulk is involved.

Why this becomes a structural scope

Equipment replacement can change support reactions, anchor geometry, dunnage layout, service clearances, and how loads enter the existing building. That is why rooftop mechanical work often needs building-side structural coordination.
