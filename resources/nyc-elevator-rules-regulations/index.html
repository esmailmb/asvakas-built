NYC Elevator Rules and Regulations

A practical resource on permits, DOB NOW resources, code references, and why elevator work often requires building-side structural coordination.

Official starting points

The public starting points are DOB's elevator and elevator permit pages, the DOB NOW: Build elevator resources, and the active 2022 Construction Codes.

Where structure enters the scope

Elevator projects frequently intersect with slab openings, hoistway modifications, pit work, support steel, receiving-structure review, or new support reactions. That is where the building-side structural engineer becomes part of the coordination team.

Keep the role definition clear

Asvakas acts as the structural engineering consultant for the building-side scope. Installation, maintenance, testing, and licensed elevator trade duties remain with the appropriate elevator contractor and other required parties.
