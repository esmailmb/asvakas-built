NYC Roof Guard and Balcony Railing Code Guide

A high-level guide to where guard requirements, parapets, guard loading, permits, and building-side structural coordination meet in NYC.

Core code themes

Roof guards and balcony railings sit at the intersection of life-safety geometry, structural loading, receiving-structure review, parapet conditions, and permit or inspection coordination. That is why they should not be treated as fabrication-only packages.

Public starting points

The most useful public anchors are the active 2022 Construction Codes, DOB's DOB NOW: Build, the Parapets page, and DOB's Special Inspections guidance when anchors or steel details are involved.
