Historic Building Opening Consultation

Use this page when a landmarked or historic building needs opening work and the team wants preservation-conscious structural coordination before the scope is fixed.

Go to Contact FormBack to Historic Opening Service

Helpful intake items

  • Landmark or historic status context
  • Architectural drawings and photos
  • Proposed window, door, or storefront changes
  • Known LPC or DOB coordination issues

What this consultation is for

The goal is to understand structural feasibility, retained historic material, likely hidden support conditions, and how the opening change should be framed before LPC-sensitive details or contractor assumptions move too far ahead.
