Roofing Structural Consultation

Use this page when the project team needs building-side structural input for roof replacement, deck deterioration, openings, parapets, or rooftop accessory loads in NYC.

Go to Contact FormBack to Roofing Service

Helpful intake items

  • Existing drawings and roof plans
  • Site photos and exposed conditions
  • Known leaks, deterioration, or movement
  • Target schedule and filing status

What to send before we speak

The most useful starting package is simple: roof plans, recent photos, a description of the intended work, and any known structural concerns such as deck replacement, parapet deterioration, openings, or new rooftop support conditions.

Common consultation topics

Replacement and reroofing

Whether deteriorated deck, hidden conditions, or changed roof geometry turn replacement into a structural scope.

Openings and parapets

Roof hatches, curbs, skylights, parapets, and edge conditions that require more than standard roofing detailing.

Rooftop add-ons

Loads from equipment, rails, solar, screens, and other accessory items added during or after roof work.
