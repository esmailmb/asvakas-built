Support Frame and Anchor Consultation

Use this page when dunnage, support steel, post-installed anchors, hangers, or receiving-structure constraints need to be defined before the scope reaches permit or fabrication.

Helpful intake items

  • Equipment weights and layouts
  • Existing substrate or framing information
  • Anchor assumptions or product notes
  • Known field or filing constraints

What this consultation is for

These scopes are often decided too late. The purpose of the consultation is to identify whether the receiving structure, edge distance, geometry, or altered equipment support condition needs a more defined structural path before procurement or filing.
