Rooftop Equipment Structural Consultation

Use this page when rooftop equipment, replacement units, dunnage, anchors, screens, or catwalks need building-side structural review before filing or fabrication.

Go to Contact FormBack to Rooftop HVAC Service

Helpful intake items

  • Unit data and weight information
  • Existing roof plans and photos
  • Dunnage or screen concepts
  • Known filing or schedule constraints

What this consultation is for

The goal is to clarify the receiving structure, whether the existing support concept can be reused, and whether dunnage, anchors, screens, catwalks, or other rooftop items should be included in the same structural scope from the start.
