Canada Rooftop Equipment Structural FAQ

Common questions about model codes, adoption timelines, climate loads, municipal permits, and authorization limits for rooftop equipment support in Canada.

Common questions

Is there one Canada-wide permit path for rooftop equipment?

No. Canada uses model codes that provinces and territories adopt on their own timelines, and municipal permitting remains local.

Why do climate loads matter so much?

Snow, wind, and roof-performance conditions can vary strongly by city, which changes how the rooftop support strategy should be approached.

Can a firm market service everywhere in Canada automatically?

No. Engineering practice authority is province-specific, so public-facing service language should stay aligned with actual authorization or local collaboration requirements.
