Roof Dunnage and Rooftop HVAC FAQ

Common questions about rooftop units, dunnage, screens, catwalks, anchors, and building-side support review in NYC.

Common questions

Does every new rooftop unit need structural review?

No. But structural review becomes more important when the work changes support reactions, dunnage geometry, anchors, layout, or the receiving structure beneath the roof.

Do screens and catwalks belong in the same structural package?

Often yes. They can change wind demand, load paths, and access assumptions enough that they should be coordinated with the equipment support scope.

What if the existing roof framing is uncertain?

That is one of the main reasons for an early structural review. Existing conditions often control the real project risk.
