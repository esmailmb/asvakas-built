What we do

Timber and wood systems require careful attention to load paths, bearing details, moisture exposure, long-term movement, and connection behavior. Asvakas helps teams evaluate framing concepts, localized support conditions, repair scopes, and interfaces where timber must coordinate with concrete, steel, or existing building fabric.

The service applies both to new framing work and to older structures where deterioration, connection issues, or modified loading conditions require technical review. It is especially useful where wood framing intersects with exterior exposure, deck conditions, rooftop work, or renovation scopes that change how loads are carried.

Applications and project types

Typical applications include wood-framed additions, deck and terrace structures, joist support review, timber connection detailing, rooftop framing modifications, and evaluation of existing wood members affected by moisture or deterioration. It also supports projects where openings, new equipment, or rehabilitation work alter the framing assumptions of a wood structure.

Why it matters

Wood systems often fail locally before the larger framing concept appears obviously deficient. Connection issues, poorly resolved bearing conditions, water exposure, and concealed deterioration can all change capacity in ways that are not apparent from surface review alone. Technical consulting helps teams define where repair, reinforcement, or reconfiguration is actually needed.

FAQ

Is this only for large timber structures?

No. It applies equally to smaller framing modifications, decks, rooftop supports, and localized wood-framing concerns.

Can it help with deteriorated wood members?

Yes. Existing-condition review and repair strategy for moisture-affected or deteriorated wood are common triggers for this service.
